Expert Guidance

Expert Guidance, Personalized to You

Ground adapts to your nervous system state, your goals, and your biometric data. Every session is selected and sequenced to meet you exactly where you are. Built from years of breathwork practice and coaching by Collin Chartier.

Nervous System Analysis

Ground reads your current state through emotional check-ins and biometric data from connected devices, building a picture of your nervous system in real time.

Personalized Prescriptions

Based on how you feel and what your body data reveals, Ground recommends the exact technique, duration, and intensity that will serve you best right now.

Adaptive Progression

Your practice evolves with you. As your capacity grows, Ground introduces more advanced techniques and longer sessions to keep deepening your practice.

Data Export
