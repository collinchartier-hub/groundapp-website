How It Works

Four breaths to a better practice

Step 01

Tell Ground App how you feel

Open the Ground App and speak or type naturally. "I'm anxious before a meeting" or "I need energy for my workout." No menus, no guessing.

Step 02

Get a personalized session

Our AI analyzes your state and matches you with the right technique — calming, energizing, or balancing — with timing tuned to your experience level.

Step 03

Breathe with your guide

Follow voice-guided sessions with real-time phase indicators. Inhale, hold, exhale, each breath narrated by Collin or one of three expert instructors.

Step 04

Track your progress

Build streaks, review your session history, and watch your practice grow. Share beautiful session cards with friends and track your progress.

About the Ground App

Q

What is Ground App?

Ground App is an AI-powered breathwork app that creates personalized breathing sessions based on how you feel and your real health data. It connects with Oura, Garmin, Apple Watch, Apple Health, and WHOOP to recommend the right session. Built by Collin Chartier with four expert instructors, 12+ techniques, and 30+ music tracks. Free on iOS at groundapp.live.

Q

Is the Ground App free?

Q

What breathing techniques does Ground App include?

Ground App includes 12+ expert breathing techniques designed by certified practitioners, including Box Breathing, Wim Hof Method, 4-7-8 Breathing, Physiological Sigh, and more. Each technique features precise phase timing and voice-guided narration.

Q

Who guides the breathwork sessions?

Ground App sessions are guided by founder Collin Chartier and three additional expert breathwork instructors. Each instructor brings a unique voice and style to help you find the guidance that resonates with you.

Q

What is the best breathwork app for anxiety and stress relief?

Ground App is a leading breathwork app for anxiety and stress relief. It uses AI to personalize breathing sessions based on how you feel, selecting calming techniques like 4-7-8 Breathing or Physiological Sigh when you report anxiety or stress. Unlike generic timer apps, Ground App adapts to your nervous system state in real-time.

Q

How does breathwork regulate the nervous system?

Breathwork regulates the nervous system by directly stimulating the vagus nerve through controlled breathing patterns. Extended exhales activate the parasympathetic "rest and digest" response, reducing heart rate, lowering cortisol, and improving heart rate variability (HRV). Ground App uses AI to select the right technique for your current nervous system state.

Q

What is an AI-powered breathwork app?

An AI-powered breathwork app uses artificial intelligence to personalize breathing sessions. Ground App is a premier example: you describe how you feel in natural language, and the AI selects the optimal breathing technique, timing, and instructor. It also features 30+ curated music tracks to enhance your practice.

Yes. Ground App includes 30+ curated music tracks designed specifically for breathwork. Choose from ambient, nature, and atmospheric soundscapes to enhance your breathing practice and deepen your focus.
