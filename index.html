What Makes Ground Different

Your practice, personalized

Built by founder and breathwork instructor Collin Chartier, Ground uses AI and voice technology to create breathwork sessions that adapt to your state, your goals, and even your own voice.

AI-Powered Personalization

Tell Ground how you feel, and our AI creates the perfect breathing session — technique, timing, and narration tailored to your nervous system.

Clone Your Voice

Record 30 seconds. Ground clones your voice and guides you through sessions in your own words. The most personal breathwork experience ever built.

12+ Expert Techniques

From Box Breathing to Wim Hof, each protocol is designed by certified practitioners with precise phase timing and voice-guided narration.

How It Works

Four breaths to a better practice

Step 01

Tell us how you feel

Open Ground and speak or type naturally. "I'm anxious before a meeting" or "I need energy for my workout." No menus, no guessing.

Step 02

Get a personalized session

Our AI analyzes your state and matches you with the right technique — calming, energizing, or balancing — with timing tuned to your experience level.

Step 03

Breathe with your guide

Follow voice-guided sessions with real-time phase indicators. Inhale, hold, exhale — each breath narrated by a soothing voice, or your own clone.

Step 04

Track your progress

Build streaks, review your session history, and watch your practice grow. Share sessions with the community and climb the leaderboard.
