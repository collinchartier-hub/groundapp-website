How It Works

Four breaths to a better practice

Step 01

Tell us how you feel

Open Ground and speak or type naturally. "I'm anxious before a meeting" or "I need energy for my workout." No menus, no guessing.

Step 02

Get a personalized session

Our AI analyzes your state and matches you with the right technique — calming, energizing, or balancing — with timing tuned to your experience level.

Step 03

Breathe with your guide

Follow voice-guided sessions with real-time phase indicators. Inhale, hold, exhale — each breath narrated by a soothing voice, or your own clone.

Step 04

Track your progress

Build streaks, review your session history, and watch your practice grow. Share sessions with the community and climb the leaderboard.
